Rewards, Deflation & Compounding

Tokens deposited in the Vault will be available to create daily rewards but will no longer be withdrawable. Deposits into the vault are burnt out of circulating supply (adding deflation) and your account receives a deposit balance (up to 360% of the initial principal amount or 100k max payout through compounding)

Our smart contract will allow you to interact with the vaults 3 times a month, in 3 different cycles:

  1. 1. after 10 days you can compound 5% of your Vault
  2. 2. after 20 days you can compound 12.5% of your Vault
  3. 3. after 30 days you can compound 25% of your Vault

After completing all 3 cycles the daily rewards restart from cycle 1 (5% after 10 days).
